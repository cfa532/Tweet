Developer: cfa532  ·  App: ZZ (Decentralized Social Media)

Overview

ZZ is a decentralized social media application built on the Leither network. Because your account data is self-hosted across your own node and those of your followers, the deletion process differs slightly from a centralised service. This page explains exactly how to request deletion and what happens to your data.

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Open the ZZ app and sign in to the account you wish to delete.
  2. Go to Profile → Settings → Account and tap "Delete Account". Follow the on-screen confirmation steps.
  3. If you cannot access the app, send a deletion request by email (see below) with the subject line Account Deletion Request and include your registered username or node ID.
  4. You will receive a confirmation within 7 business days. Deletion is completed within 30 days of your confirmed request.
Email: cfa532@gmail.com

What Data Is Deleted

The table below summarises each category of data, whether it is deleted, and any applicable retention period.

Data Category Status Retention / Notes
Account credentials (username, password hash) Deleted Removed within 30 days of confirmed request
Profile information (display name, avatar, bio) Deleted Removed within 30 days of confirmed request
Posts / tweets you authored Deleted Removed from your node immediately. Copies cached on followers' nodes are purged within 30 days as the network propagates the deletion signal.
Media files (photos, videos, audio) you uploaded Deleted Removed from your node within 30 days; cached copies purged as above
Direct messages Deleted Your copy is deleted immediately. The recipient's copy is theirs to manage and is not deleted.
Notifications and activity logs Deleted Removed within 30 days
Aggregated, anonymised analytics Retained Non-identifiable usage statistics (e.g. total active nodes) may be retained indefinitely as they cannot be linked back to any individual account.
Transaction / reward records Retained up to 1 year Records required for platform revenue-sharing audits are retained for up to 12 months after account deletion, then permanently removed.
Decentralised note: ZZ operates on a peer-to-peer network. While your node and our infrastructure will delete all data within 30 days, we cannot guarantee immediate removal from third-party nodes outside our network that may have cached your public content before the deletion request was made.

Partial Deletion (Data Portability)

If you only want to delete specific content rather than your entire account, you can delete individual posts, profile details, or uploaded media from within the app without closing your account.
