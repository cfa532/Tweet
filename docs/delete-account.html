Developer: cfa532

How to Request Deletion

  1. Open the dTweet app and go to Settings → Delete Account.
  2. Confirm the deletion when prompted.
  3. If you cannot access the app, email cfa532@yahoo.com with the subject Account Deletion Request and include your username.

Data Deleted

  • Account credentials, profile information
  • Posts, media (photos, videos), comments
  • Activity logs and notifications

All data above is permanently deleted within 30 days of your confirmed request.

Data Retained

DataRetention Period
Transaction / reward records Up to 12 months (for platform audit purposes), then deleted
Anonymised aggregate analytics Indefinitely (cannot be linked to any individual)
